About

Hi, I'm Jensine Morzo

A self-taught painter who uses acrylic, oil, watercolor, and charcoal to create unique and captivating art pieces. My passion for art began in childhood, but it wasn't until later in life that I discovered my talent for painting. Before dedicating myself to canvas painting, I worked in the Middle East designing clothing, interiors, and furniture, which allowed me to develop a keen eye for design.

My art has been exhibited globally, including in Belgium, India, Lebanon, Egypt, and the Philippines, and has been sold to royalties and VIPs. I'm constantly learning and exploring new techniques and styles, striving to create works that are visually stunning and thought-provoking. Through my art, I hope to inspire others and share my love for the beauty and complexity of the world around us.
